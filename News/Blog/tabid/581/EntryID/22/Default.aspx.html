Divorced Dad brings test case to publicize shared parenting deal - Dad wants to set example to other former couples: Injunction bans him from revealing details of his parenting plan.

Divorced Dad brings test case to publicize shared parenting deal - Dad wants to set example to other former couples: Injunction bans him from revealing details of his parenting plan.

Imagine being banned from sharing something helpful to other children - their parents settling matters and agreeing on sharing custody equally. Thats government lunacy at its worst. But incredibly a leading UK media lawyer is representing the father.


"The father, a campaigner for fathers' rights and a volunteer adviser to other divorced and separated men, wants to show the parenting plan to others as an example of what can be achieved, even in cases where there is conflict."

That's what e wants to do - but he needs a Court order to do it.

We do not offer legal advice at our support group meetings. Legal advice can only be obtained from a lawyer. We do not have a lawyer in attendance at our support group meetings.
