Our regular weekly 70 minute teleseminars begin tonight

=====================================
BACKGROUND INFO ON THE
DIVORCED DAD WEEKLY TELESEMINAR
=====================================

The entire system is still not set up, however we

to purchase a transcript and/or audio of these
weekly calls. For the meantime there will be a FREE
replay page fort each call set up until we get the system
finished.

HOW TO LISTEN IN AND / OR
PARTICIPATE LIVE ON THE TELESEMINAR
=====================================

You will be able to ask questions on the call also
through the user interface...

TONIGHT'S TELESEMINAR TRAINING:
=====================================

Divorce 101 - Step One - Invigoration.

Heidi and I will be giving a short introductory talk on
each call, followed by a Q & A session. We encourage
you to use the Divorced Dad Weekly web page interface
to send us any questions you have during the training at:

http://www.DivorcedDadWeekly.com

================================
MORE BACKGROUND ON THESE
TELESEMINARS
================================

For those of you who don’t live near a meeting location,
or can’t make a meeting, this newest outreach project is
a GREAT solution for those who can't attend a meeting,
either because of time constraints, or distance.

Those who attend will get in on some really great offers
on new products and services we will be offering to help
you win in Family Court on these calls from time to time.

For those of you who have met me at a meeting, you
already well know how many I’ve helped in these meetings
with simple suggestions, strategies and tips.

And for those of you on the calls, you will get some
really amazing opportunities to turn your situation
around.

If we have never spoken or met before this will be an
opportunity for you to learn from myself, and my co-host
for these calls, Heidi Nabert.

Heidi will bring a enormous amount of knowledge on your
children - especially those of you who have daughters.
Like me, she is also an adult child of divorce.

When we begin tonight's call at 7:30 EST, the lines will be
open for people to introduce themselves and where they
are calling from.

At 7:40 PM EST , we will begin with an introduction of who we are, followed by a short break to welcome callers and then resume at 8 pm EST with our training session on tonight's topic.

The ideas are to help you expand your awareness of
possible new approaches and solutions to difficult
problems, that are based upon the successful strategies
of the many fathers we have helped over the years and
an ENORMOUS amount of research.

================================
ASKING QUESTIONS LIVE DURING
THIS WEEKS TELESEMINAR
================================

Once the training is over, we will take questions through
the Divorced Dad Weekly  interface as follows...

FIRST COME, FIRST SERVED.

NOTE: If it is possible to open the phone lines and teach
ALL participants to "mute" and "unmute" their individual
lines, we will have "A LIVE CALL-IN SESSION"

However, we must do so in an orderly way for it to work
well. Therefore we have a procedure to follow to keep it
orderly.

Consider it a test of your ability to follow instructions
successfully and to work as a team on the call.

We will have some time to practice this procedure on our
calls, so you can get it right. Once you learn it - IT'S EASY.

This procedure is ABSOLUTELY necessary to keep
background noise down on the call, and to make for an
"easy to listen to call / webcast and / or recording of the
call / webcast.

TELESEMINAR PROCEDURE
================================

We will take Questions in the order we receive them.
Ask your question on the Divorced dad Weekly
interface at:

http://www.DivorcedDadWeekly.com

We will also take a "roll call for questions" during
the Q & A session. If we have a lively session with a
lot of good participation we may go beyond the 90
minutes as a REWARD for taking POSITIVE STEPS
& ACTION towards solving your difficulties.

the call when we open the telephone for all participants
and the live Q & A Session, YOU MUST:

PRESS *6.

TO UN-MUTE YOUR PHONE LINE [First Name], on
the call when we open the telephone for all participants
and the live Q & A Session, YOU MUST:

PRESS *7.

**IMPORTANT NOTICE ABOUT SUPPORT GROUP MEETINGS**
We do not offer legal advice at our support group meetings. Legal advice can only be obtained from a lawyer. We do not have a lawyer in attendance at our support group meetings. Our free support group meetings are chaired by a senior certified law clerk who is also a divorced father and an adult child of divorce. Our Support Group meetings are a place to share and exchange personal experience and knowledge about the process of separation and divorce. Discussions at our meetings are of a supportive nature so that fathers can gain a clearer understanding of separation/divorce from the perspective of fathers who have had success in Family Court. Meeting dates are subject to change.
